Shall I explain the flow of battle?<$FFFE>
Each battle has a prologue, then a preparation screen where you hire troops, buy items, equip and change class.<$FFFD>
Deploy your commanders and choose Begin Battle.<$FFFD>
Watch the battlefield and enemy positions when placing units.<$FFFD>
Battles occur on the map and in Battle mode.<$FFFD>
Each scenario has victory and defeat conditions.<$FFFC>
Meet the victory conditions to advance the story. Defeat means game over.<$FFFD>
Conditions are shown in the prologue and can change mid-scenario. Check them via the main menu.<$FFFE>
Want to learn about hiring troops?<$FFFE>
One troop type per hire. Troops improve along with their commander.<$FFFD>
Press L and R to cycle commanders on the recruit screen.<$FFFD>
Hold L on the map to see HP. Press Start or Z to open the menu.<$FFFE>
Want to learn about the battle system?<$FFFE>
This game has a new battle system. Orders go to commanders only, not individual soldiers.<$FFFD>
Commands are not carried out immediately. Press Start and select Execute Turn to have all units act at once.<$FFFE>
Shall I explain the commands?<$FFFE>
[Move] Move units on the map. A highlighted area shows movement range. You can set a route.<$FFFD>
[Recover] Recover HP. The unit cannot move or attack that turn.<$FFFD>
[Magic] Cast spells using MP. Stronger spells take longer. Unit cannot act otherwise.<$FFFD>
[Item] Equip or use items. Only one per turn, unit cannot act otherwise.<$FFFD>
[Use] Activate special item effects. Some items may break.<$FFFD>
[Summon] Spend MP to call an ally creature. One summon per character. Treat it like a commander.<$FFFE>
[Skill] Some skills are passive, others can be activated in battle.<$FFFD>
Combine commands to cover weaknesses and boost strengths.<$FFFC>
Mode and Formation are explained next.<$FFFD>
You can change commands freely until you select Execute Turn.<$FFFE>
Want to learn about unit modes?<$FFFE>
Three modes: Normal, High Mobility, and Defense.<$FFFD>
Normal is balanced. High Mobility moves farther but lowers attack and defense.<$FFFD>
Defense stops movement but raises defense. Units must move to change formation.<$FFFE>
Want to learn about formations?<$FFFE>
Formations set how soldiers line up around their commander. Changes apply after moving.<$FFFD>
Square: soldiers ring the commander.<$FFFD>
Spear: reach distant enemies in a line.<$FFFD>
Wall: block front and back.<$FFFD>
Slant: two variants for flexible angles.<$FFFD>
Pick formations to control which units engage.<$FFFE>
Want to learn about battle mode?<$FFFE>
Battle starts when units make contact or are in range. Even one soldier connecting brings the whole unit into battle.<$FFFD>
Mind your formation to avoid exposing weak units.<$FFFD>
As the attacker, you choose which enemy to strike, or do nothing.<$FFFE>
Use the D-pad to cycle targets. A 3D battle scene plays when units clash.<$FFFD>
You can give orders to commanders and squads in this scene.<$FFFD>
To skip it, set Battle Scenes to off in Game Options. Results are then calculated automatically.<$FFFE>
